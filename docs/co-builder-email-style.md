## Do's & Don'ts

**Do:**
- Keep under 300 words (excluding tables)
- Name specific people for contributions
- Show before/after when scores change
- Give concrete examples (good bug report vs vague one)
- End with clear action item

**Don't:**
- List every technical detail (they don't need commit hashes)
- Repeat the same CTA from last email verbatim
- Use English jargon without context
- Send without proofreading (names, numbers)
- Mention Yuma or GLS-ERP topics to GIS-ERP team
